My Top Three Favorite Movies

The Hunger Games

The Hunger Games is a gritty but vivid five-movie series. It is closely based on the popular dystopian book series. The actors do a stunning job of bringing the colorful characters to life, and the director captures the feel of the series perfectly. It is one of the most realistic dystopia movies ever made.

Star Wars

The Star Wars Saga is well-known for a reason. The characters are enjoyable, the world elaborate, and the plot line complex and entertaining. Even the soundtrack has become legendary, as have the classic characters; the movies have become so deeply ingrained in our culture that we cannot imagine a world without the galaxy far, far away.

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter movies are a very well done adaptation of the wildly popular book series. The actors are all very talented and represent the characters better than any of J.K. Rowling's numerous fans could have ever asked for. These movies are a must-watch for fantasy lovers in any stage of life.
